From Here to Home Site Plan

Thomas Garrett

WDD 131-A1

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a place for my family members to share and veiw memories we have together or while traveling.

Audience

The main audience of this website is going to be my family. It may be beneificial for others to look at if they wish to travel somewhere my family has shared on the site.

Dynamic elements

The dynamic elements of the website will be: An array of vacations or memories that hold dates, descriptions, images, etc. Filtering will allow vacations or memories with certain conditions to be viewed like date, location, or category. Memories with more than a single image attached will have the ability to click through by pressing arrows or if the image is clicked they will be able to scroll through all the images. A light/dark mode toggle. There will also be condtional logic that will show seasonal messages and also allow the user to mark a favorite trip, which will change the appearance of the memory.
